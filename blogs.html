Understanding Cyber Laws in Nepal

Cyber laws play a crucial role in regulating online activities and protecting digital rights. In Nepal, these laws are especially important as we continue to embrace digital transformation.

The Electronic Transaction Act (ETA) of Nepal serves as the primary legislation governing electronic transactions and cybercrime. This comprehensive framework addresses various aspects of digital security, including:

  • Digital signatures and authentication
  • Cybercrime prevention and punishment
  • Protection of data privacy
  • Regulation of electronic transactions
