Understanding Cyber Laws in Nepal

Overview of Cyber Laws in Nepal Cyber laws in Nepal, primarily governed by the Electronic Transactions Act (ETA), 2063 (2008), regulate digital activities, ensuring secure online transactions, data privacy, and intellectual property protection. The ETA legalizes digital signatures, criminalizes cybercrimes like hacking, and recognizes electronic records as valid evidence. These laws aim to create a safe digital ecosystem for individuals and organizations.
